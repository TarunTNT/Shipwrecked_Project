Reverse Wordle

You see a fully solved Wordle (6 guesses) but only colors remain.
Click any tile to guess its hidden letter, then press Enter.
You get three tries per tile (five in Forgiving mode).
After your first miss, a grey number shows how many tries remain.
Use the helper tools on the right to deduce each letter. Please note that the Phantom Scan tells you what letters are in the puzzle, NOT what letters are in the solved word.
